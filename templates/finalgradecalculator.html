Frequently Asked Questions

How does this calculator work?

Calculator is formula use karta hai: Final Grade Required = (Desired Grade - (Current Grade * Remaining Weight)) / Final Exam Weight. Is tarah yeh aapke final exam ke liye zaroori score batata hai.

Is this calculator accurate?

Jee bilkul. Yeh calculator standard weighted average formula ka istemal karta hai. Bas yeh yaqeen kar lein ke aapne sahi values daali hain.

Can I use it for any course?

Jee haan, aap is calculator ko kisi bhi course ke liye istemal kar sakte hain, bas aapko us course ka current grade, final exam ka weight, aur apna desired grade pata hona chahiye.
