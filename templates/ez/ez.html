Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this easy grade calculator for any type of test?

Absolutely! It works for quizzes, homework, class tests, or final exams of any size, from small 10-question quizzes to large 200-question exams.

Can this grading calculator handle multiple students at once?

No, it calculates one score at a time. Teachers can quickly repeat the process for multiple students, saving hours of grading work compared to manual calculations.

Is it possible to adjust the grading scale on this calculator to align with my school’s system?

Yes. By using the “Set Grade Scale” option, you can customize the percentage thresholds for each letter grade. This is especially useful for schools with unique grading systems or plus/minus scales (e.g., A-, B+, etc.).

What happens if a student gets all the answers correct?

If the “Total Wrong” is zero, our calculator will automatically display a 100% score, instantly confirming a perfect result on the test or assignment.
