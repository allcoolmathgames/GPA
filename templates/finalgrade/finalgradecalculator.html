What Is the Final Grade Calculator?

This final grade calculator helps students determine the score they need on remaining assignments or exams to achieve a desired overall course grade. It quickly calculates the required marks based on current performance and the weight of remaining work.

Final Grade Calculation: Formula and Example

Formula:
Final Grade = (Current Grade × Completed Work Weight) + (Desired Final Exam Score × Remaining Work Weight)

Where:
Current Grade = Your average score so far in the course
Completed Work Weight = Percentage of the course already graded
Remaining Work Weight = Percentage of the course grade still to be completed (e.g., final exam)

Example:

Current Grade: 85%
Completed Work Weight: 70%
Final Exam Weight: 30%
Desired Final Grade: 90%

Formula:

90 = ( 85 x 0.70 ) + ( Required Exam Score x 0.30 )

Step-by-Step:

  1. 85 x 0.70 = 59.5
  2. 90 - 59.5 = 30.5
  3. 30.5 ÷ 0.30 = 101.67

Result: You’d need about 102% on the final exam to reach a 90% final grade, which means it’s mathematically impossible unless there’s extra credit available.
