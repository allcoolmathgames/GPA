How to Use the Grade Calculator

Step 1: Enter Your Assessments

List each assessment (e.g., quizzes, midterms, final exam) and its score (in percentage).

Step 2: Add Weights

Enter the percentage weight of each assessment. The total weight can be 100% or any other value.

Step 3: Click Calculate Grade

The calculator will instantly show your total weighted grade.

What Is a Grade Calculator?

Grade Calculator ek online tool hai jo students ko unke grades ko calculate karne mein madad karta hai. Is tool mein, aap apne assessments (jaise ke quizzes, assignments, midterms, aur final exams) ke scores aur unke weights enter karte hain. Iske baad, calculator in values ko use karke aapka **weighted average grade** nikalti hai.

Use Cases Of This Calculator

  • **Semester-wise Planning:** Aap isse har semester ki shuruat mein apne grades ka target set karne ke liye istemal kar sakte hain.
  • **Final Exam Planning:** Jab aapko pata chal jae ke aapko final exam mein kitne marks chahiye to aap uske mutabiq apni preparation kar sakte hain.
  • **Progress Tracking:** Aap isse apne academic progress ko monitor karne aur yeh dekhne ke liye istemal kar sakte hain ke aap apne goals ki taraf kitni tezi se progress kar rahe hain.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Grade Calculator work?

This calculator works on the principle of weighted average. It multiplies each assessment's score by its weight and then divides the sum of these products by the total weight.

Is it necessary for the total weight to be 100%?

No, it is not necessary. The calculator will find your weighted average grade even if the total weight is not 100%.

Can I use this for multiple courses at once?

This calculator is designed for one course at a time. To calculate grades for a different course, simply reset the fields or refresh the page.
